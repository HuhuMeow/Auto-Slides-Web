Auto-Slides

Interactive multi-agent system for creating and customizing research presentations.

Problem and Motivation

  • LLM chat lacks global structure for systematic paper understanding.
  • Research presentations require narrative and visual grounding.
  • Auto-Slides converts papers into pedagogically organized Beamer slides.

Multi-Agent Pipeline

  • Parser and Planner Agents structure paper content.
  • Verification and Adjustment Agents improve fidelity.
  • Generator and Editor Agents produce and refine slides.
